The Lion

The lion is a mammal native to Africa. They have yellow-brown fur, and tails. The males have manes, but the females don't. Males are also larger then females, being about 72 to 82 inches long Lions are feline, and live in packs unlike it's relatives. It's an apex predator on the safari. The population declined by about 43% in the 1990s.
